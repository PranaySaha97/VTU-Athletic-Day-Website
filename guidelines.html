GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS


  • The detailed entries should send to this E-mail ID: [sjcitvtuathletics2018@gmail.com] before 10th October 2018.
  • A meeting of all the managers is convened on 25th October 2018 at 4.00pm in SJCIT campus.
  • Name of athletes should be written in block letters.
  • An athlete can take part in any two events and in any one of the relays (as per AIU norms). If an athlete fills for more than two events, the other events will not be considered.
  • There shall not be more than two participants in each event from each college. If more than two participants are there in any team, first two will only be considered.
  • Transportation will be provided from Chikkaballapura city bus stand and city railway station on 25th October 2018 from 6.00am onwards.
  • Original Eligibility Performa duly singed by the principal should be submit at the Registration Counter.
  • Institution I.D. Cards is compulsory for all the athletes.
  • Unfinished accommodation facilities will be provided to the participants and team Managers / Coaches / Physical Education Directors.
  • The registration will start from 25th October 2018 from 9.00am onwards.
  • It is mandatory for all the participating teams / athletes and Physical Education Directors should report 30minutes prior to the march-past with college flag.
  • Inaugural function starts at 9.30am on 26th October 2018.
  • To conduct any event Minimum 04 athletes / 04 relay teams is must.
  • Points for individual events: 1st place 05 points, 2nd place 03 points, and 3rd place 01points.
  • Points for relay: 1st place 10 points, 2nd place 06 points, and 3rd place 03points.
  • Caution deposit of Rs.1000/- has to be paid separately by the both men and women team which is refundable.
  • Teams should bring their own chains, lock and key. Because they may have to share the rooms with more teams and it also advised not to carry valuable materials like gold, costly mobile, camera and cash. Organizer is not responsible for any loss of belongings.
